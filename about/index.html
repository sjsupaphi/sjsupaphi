About Us

Pi Alpha Phi is an Asian American fraternity that was founded in 1926. Decades later, in 1991, our chapter's founding fathers established roots at this university, paving the way for the growth of Delta Chapter. Since that point, our chapter has developed and shaped itself into a respected entity of the campus community. Pi Alpha Phi holds five specific pillars above all.

assignment

Academic Excellence

Our brothers strive to excel and uphold a scholarly standard with the highest regard to academics. We continually stress the value of putting learning and grades above all and have a strict GPA policy for all members and pledges.

rss_feed

Asian American Awareness

As members of an exceptionally diverse community, we stay true to our roots, and hold value in the culture our founding fathers developed this fraternity from. Our organization welcomes all ethnicities and advocates diversity.

group

Brotherhood

Arguable our most important pillar, from which we have built the foundation of our fraternity, ultimately transforming a concept into a reality. Every semester, every year, our brotherhood expands. Through a highly selective process we choose potential members based on their quality and characteristics that we might welcome as a beneficial addition to our brotherhood.

gavel

Leadership

Our organization actively promotes the assimilation of our brothers into leadership positions which reflects as our community's role models. Being a part of the Greek community opens opportunities for individuals to step up and shine.

favorite

Philanthropy

To give back to the community, we participate in various activities helping other organizations in need. Some notable philanthropies include assisting Barry Chang when he was running for the Mayor of Cupertino, our annual Jade Ribbon Campaign, and our semester AADP Bone Marrow Donation.
